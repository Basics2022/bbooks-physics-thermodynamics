$$S_x := T \dfrac{\partial S}{\partial T}\Big|_x$$

**Coefficienti di espansione termica.**

$$\alpha_x := \dfrac{1}{v} \dfrac{\partial v}{\partial T}\Big|_x = - \dfrac{1}{\rho} \dfrac{\partial \rho}{\partial T}\Big|_x$$
